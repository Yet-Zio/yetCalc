Calculator with basic and scientific calculation features, a unit converter and a programmer mode.
It's design is inspired by Google's Calculator and tries to address its flaws.

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
<li>Progressive Calculations.</li>
<li>Scientific Mode with trigonometric, hyperbolic and inverse functions, calculus and iterated operators, much more.</li>
<li>Converter of units with selectable unit groups, unit pins, search and much more.</li>
<li>Programmer Mode with number systems and logical operations.</li>
<li>Scientific mode works in both Portrait and Landscape mode.</li>
<li>The starting mode is selectable.</li>
<li>Shortcuts for home screen with themed icons support.</li>
<li>Abyss mode (AMOLED Dark) for those wanting to preserve battery life.</li>
<li>Support of Material You design and Dynamic Colors, with 4 unique styles to serve your needs.</li>
<li>Endless customizability.</li>
</ul>

Internet connection is used for only currency conversions and nothing else.
